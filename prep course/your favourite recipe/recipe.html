Butter Chicken Pizza

O.k., so maybe that header isn't butter chicken pizza, but it got you hungry, right? And there's nothing quite like butter chicken after a day of fresh powder or, as is often the case, the fresh hell of pure ice (hello rockies!). Butter chicken warms the soul and makes a great hot pack for your sure-to-be-bruised tummy. But what's the best way to get the butter chicken into, as Swede's often say, your mouth hole? Butter chicken pizza!

By the time you're done that last slice, you're not going to want to eat butter chicken any other way.

Ingredients: What you need

The Sauce

  • One small can of tomato sauce
  • Two teaspoons each of the following spices:
    • garam masala, chili powder, fenugreek leaves*
  • One teaspoon of a great cook's best friend, salt
  • Two teaspoons each of crushed ginger and garlic
  • Half of one can of coconut milk
  • A squirt of lemon juice
  • Two teaspoons of something sweet, like honey

The Toppings (and one Bottoming)

  • One pizza crust (pre-made or fresh, your call)
  • Chicken - breast is best, sliced is nice (pulled is good too but doesn't rhyme)
  • Julienned green onions
  • Sliced red pepper
  • Cheese (in this case, it's great without cheese too, so feel free to exclude)
  • Spinach

*Don't worry about the Fenugreek Leaves if you don't have them on hand.

Your steps to great pizza

  1. Fry up those chicken slices with a little bit of olive oil. Sprinkle some extra chili powder on top for a kick.
  2. While that's cooking, mix tomato sauce, spices, salt, ginger, garlic, coconut milk, and honey together.
  3. Heat the sauce until warm. Mix well.
  4. Once cooked, move the chicken from pan to bowl and drizzlelemon juice over it.
  5. Slice up peppers, onion, and spinach (if you're into it).
  6. Sauce, toppings, cheese. Bake for 20-30 at 375 and enjoy!
